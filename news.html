Highlights & press

Selected milestones from the group — awards, student-led breakthroughs, and media coverage, most recent first.

  • 2026 · 06

    Group affiliated with the 2026 RSC Prize winners (CHOISE Team @ NREL, USA).

  • 2025 · 08

    Sang Woo Bae developed water-stable perovskite color-conversion patterns for microdisplays — covered by Hankyoreh, Veritas-α, Nate, and others.

  • 2024 · 12

    Prof. Kim featured for advancing next-generation energy-engineering technology to benefit society (News H).

  • 2024 · 11

    Hanyang–Sookmyung team develops next-generation blue phosphorescent LED devices — covered by UNN, Asia Times, News H, Maeil Ilbo, Newsis, and eNews Today.

  • 2024 · 06

    Sang Hyun Nam develops a chiral perovskite material with world-record asymmetry — covered by Daily Hankook, News H, Chungcheong Ilbo, and Daejeon Ilbo.

  • 2023 · 02

    Tae Hyung Kim develops a high-efficiency photocatalyst that reduces CO2 and decomposes wastewater using natural sunlight alone (News H).

  • 2022 · 05

    Highest-efficiency large-area perovskite LED reported in Nature Nanotechnology — covered by Dong-A Science and EurekAlert.
