Join the lab

We look forward to applications from excellent researchers with backgrounds in energy, materials, chemical, or electronics engineering, physics, and chemistry.

  • Automated / AI-driven high-efficiency stacked opto-semiconductor devices — displays, solar cells, photosensors, optical communication
  • Catalysis — CO2 reduction and H2 production
  • Eco-friendly semiconductor processing (CMP)

Tuition and living stipends are supported, and members are encouraged to attend domestic and international conferences and to pursue overseas study or industry careers after their degree. Email Prof. Kim to start a conversation.
